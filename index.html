About Us

"At S.V CABS, we've been driven by a passion for delivering exceptional transportation solutions across Chennai for over 25 years. Founded in 2000 in Adyar, our journey has been marked by unwavering dedication to safety, reliability, and customer satisfaction. With a diverse fleet of well-maintained vehicles and experienced drivers, we provide tailored cab services for individuals, corporations, and events. From local to outstation trips, our commitment to excellence has earned us the trust of Chennai's residents and visitors alike. As we continue to innovate and expand, our core values remain unchanged: customer-centric approach, safety above all, timely and reliable services, transparency, and continuous improvement."
